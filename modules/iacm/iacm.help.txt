## Infrastructure as Code Management (iacm)

Harness IACM manages Terraform and OpenTofu infrastructure lifecycles. It tracks
workspace state, runs plans and applies, and provides drift detection and cost
estimation across your infrastructure.

### Domain Model

A workspace is the core unit — it maps to a Terraform workspace with a specific
backend, variables, and state. Workspaces run operations (plan, apply, destroy)
which produce execution logs and state snapshots.
