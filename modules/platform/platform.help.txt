## Platform (platform)

The Platform module covers account-level and organizational resources that are shared
across all Harness modules. This includes the identity hierarchy, access control, and
the connectors and secrets that other modules depend on.

### Domain Model

The scope hierarchy is: account → organization → project. Most resources live at one
level of this hierarchy and inherit settings from above.

Access control is: user (or service_account or user_group) + role + resource_group →
role_assignment. A role bundles permissions; a resource_group scopes which resources
the role applies to.

Connectors and secrets are declared at any scope level and referenced by pipelines and
other resources. Delegates are agents that run in your infrastructure and execute tasks
on behalf of Harness.
